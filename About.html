ABOUT TSUNAMI

Brand Story and Values

Tsunami is not merely a luxury womenswear brand—it is a manifestation of transformation, strength, and grace. Born from the founder Thuy Trieu Dang Ngo’s personal journey, Tsunami embodies an unstoppable force of nature. The brand’s name resonates with the powerful energy of a rising tide—a symbol that echoes the founder’s own experiences and the enduring lessons imparted by her father.

The Power of a Name

In Vietnamese, elements of the name “Triều Dâng” evoke imagery of natural rhythms and rising tides, representing the transformative energy and resilience that define the brand. Inspired by her father’s lifelong example of courage and determination, the designer has infused Tsunami with values that celebrate overcoming adversity and embracing change. Her father’s influence shines through in every detail—from the fluid silhouettes to the resilient spirit captured in each design.
